Recipe

The above is the cheeseburger youll be making today.

The recipe we'll be using is a classic, american, cheeseburger. The Recipe was created by [My own personal knowledge of burgers], which is not very good, mind you.

Ingredients

  • Your Choice of Ground Beef
  • Your Choice of Bun
  • American Cheese
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Pepper
  • Your Choice of Condiments
  • Fresh Tomato
  • Fresh Lettuce
  • Fresh Pickle

Steps

  1. Preheat toaster to 350F.
  2. Mix The Salt & Pepper into the ground beef.
  3. Create patties out of the meat & place on wax paper.
  4. Place patties in refridgerator to allow for them to solidify their shape.
  5. Take the patties out of the refridgerator & place them in the toaster.
  6. Set timer for 15 minutes.
  7. Slice the lettuce, tomato, & pickle.
  8. Catch the Patties out of the toaster when they pop up from the toaster.
  9. Place the patties on the bun, add the condiments & toppings.
  10. Enjoy whatever the fuck you just made.
