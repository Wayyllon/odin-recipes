Chicken Sandwich Recipe

Recipe

The above is the Chicken Sandwich youll be making today.

Ingredients

  • Toaster
  • M&M's
  • White Bread
  • Mustard
  • Chicken Patty
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Preheat toaster.
  2. Season chicken patty with salt & pepper.
  3. Place chicken patty in toaster.
  4. Prepare the white bread with M&M's, Mayonnaise, & Mustard.
  5. Place chicken patty on white bread, & place the top bun
  6. Enjoy
