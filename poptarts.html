Poptart Recipe

Recipe

The above is the poptart youll be making today.

Ingredients

  • Toaster
  • Poptart
  • An Electric Source (Optional)

Steps (Cooked)

  1. Unwrap poptart.
  2. Put poptart in toaster.
  3. Plug in the toaster.
  4. Turn the power knob to three
  5. Push the toaster down.
  6. Closely monitor toaster to make sure you dont smell burning
  7. Once finished, put the poptarts on a plate. Do NOT use silverware to get the poptart out of the toaster!
  8. Enjoy
  9. Re-evaluate your life choices. (Optional)

Steps (Uncooked)

  1. Unwrap poptart.
  2. Eat the poptart.
  3. Feel your sins crawling on your hands, for not toasting it, you monster.
  4. Shame on you.
